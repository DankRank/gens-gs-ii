This copy of minizip-1.1 is a modified version of the original.

In particular, the build system has been replaced with CMake,
and the additional MiniZip tools and examples have been removed.

To obtain the original minizip-1.1, visit
http://www.winimage.com/zLibDll/minizip.html

NOTE: Some changes were imported from zlib-1.2.8's copy of
minizip-1.1. To obtain a copy of zlib-1.2.8, visit the zlib
website at http://www.zlib.net/ .
